✨ Welcome to RISHI Parent Portal

Hello, and welcome aboard! 🙏

RISHI is not just a tutorial app — it's a warm companion that sits beside your child, just like a trusted family member. Here's everything you and your child need to know.

📋 Your To-Do as a Parent
STEP 1
Create a Study Plan — Go to Study Plan tab. Pick one or more chapters from any topic you want your child to focus on.
STEP 2
Set the Date Range — RISHI will suggest ideal timelines. You can change them, but it won't allow less than the minimum time needed per chapter.
STEP 3
Set Study Time Slots — Go to the Study Slots tab. Change the predefined times to suit your child's schedule. Times are in 24-hour format.
STEP 4
Watch the Dashboard — It shows your child's activity, scores, chapter completion, and progress trends — all in one place.
STEP 5
Monitor Breaks — You'll see how many breaks your child took, how long each lasted, and what type — water, washroom, or just idle.
STEP 6
If the screen is inactive for 5 minutes, RISHI automatically marks it as a break and starts recording time — your child can't hide idle time.
STEP 7
The Live Status tab shows you in real time whether your child is online or offline — even if they close the browser, shut down the machine, or go incognito.
STEP 8
Lock the Plan — Once you're satisfied, lock it. The student portal updates in real time the moment you lock. Unlock anytime to revise.
STEP 9
After locking, brief your child on what they need to know — what chapters, what time, and what the rules are. Informed students perform better.
STEP 10
Check the Leaderboard to see how your child ranks against others — coins earned through correct answers and streaks drive the ranking.
🎓 Please Inform Your Child
KNOW 1
You can only access the chapters assigned to you, and only within the time slots your parent has set. Outside that — RISHI stays locked.
KNOW 2
Every correct answer and every streak earns you coins. These can be redeemed for real rewards — your parent can cash them out for you!
KNOW 3
Every chapter follows a strict order: Explain → Practice → Exam. You cannot skip ahead. Each step unlocks the next.
KNOW 4
Explain must be completed before Practice opens. Practice requires 5 correct answers in a row before the Chapter Exam becomes available.
KNOW 5
Every chapter has its own exam. Complete all chapter exams in a topic to unlock the Topic Exam.
KNOW 6
Pass all Topic Exams and the grand Sampurna Pariksha unlocks — the ultimate test of everything you've learned.
KNOW 7
Check the Leaderboard to see your rank among peers. Mental boosting games are coming soon to keep your brain sharp between chapters.
KNOW 8
Questions are inspired by and aligned with RD Sharma, RS Aggarwal, NCERT Exemplar, CBSE past papers, and Olympiad syllabi — reworded and adapted fresh for RISHI.
KNOW 9
Every break you take is recorded. Screen idle for 5 minutes = automatic break. Your parent can see everything. Study honestly!
KNOW 10
Beyond your set time, RISHI won't open. If something feels wrong, refresh 2–3 times with a 30-second gap. Still broken? Screenshot + WhatsApp your ID and registered number to your admin.
🤝Remember — RISHI is not just a tutorial app. It is a companion that sits beside your child like a warm, knowledgeable family member — guiding, encouraging, and holding them accountable every step of the way.
