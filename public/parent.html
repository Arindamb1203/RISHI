Your To-Do as a Parent
10 steps
+
  • ›Create a Study Plan — pick chapters in the Study Plan tab.
  • ›Set date range — RISHI suggests timelines; adjust within limits.
  • ›Set study time slots — 24-hour format; outside these, RISHI locks.
  • ›Watch Analytics — scores, completion & trends in one place.
  • ›Monitor breaks — count, length & type (water / washroom / idle).
  • ›Idle 5 min = auto break — idle time can't be hidden.
  • ›Live Status — online/offline in real time, even incognito.
  • ›Lock the plan — student portal updates instantly; unlock to revise.
  • ›Brief your child — chapters, times & rules. Informed = better.
  • ›Leaderboard — coins from correct answers & streaks drive rank.

Please Inform Your Child
10 points
+
  • ›Only assigned chapters open, only within set time slots.
  • ›Correct answers & streaks earn coins → real rewards.
  • ›Strict order: Explain → Practice → Exam. No skipping.
  • ›Practice needs 5 correct in a row to unlock the exam.
  • ›Finish all chapter exams in a topic → Topic Exam unlocks.
  • ›Pass all topics → grand Sampurna Pariksha unlocks.
  • ›Leaderboard shows your rank; brain games coming soon.
  • ›Questions aligned with NCERT, RD Sharma, Olympiad & more.
  • ›Every break is recorded — parent sees everything.
